About Me

I am Tiurma, a songwriter and independent artist from Medan, Indonesia, working within the space of alternative pop and pop folk, where storytelling becomes the core of my musical language. I began writing songs at the age of 12, not as a planned path, but as a natural response to the emotions and experiences I could not otherwise express. Since then, songwriting has remained a constant practice — sometimes disciplined, sometimes fragile, but always honest. My work is rooted in storytelling. I am drawn to songs that feel lived-in, imperfect, and human — where silence is as important as sound, and where melody carries memory. I am not only interested in writing songs, but in observing life closely enough for it to become music. Across the years, I have written hundreds of songs, many of which remain unreleased or forgotten, yet each one has shaped my understanding of voice, identity, and emotional truth. As a performer, I move between intimate storytelling and live stage energy, shaped by my experiences as a vocalist and live musician since 2010. For me, songwriting is not a product, but a continuous dialogue with memory, distance, and emotion.
